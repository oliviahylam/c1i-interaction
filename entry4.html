Red Cube by Isamu Noguchi

The Red Cube by Isamu Noguchi, installed in 1968 in front of 140 Broadway in New York City's Financial District, is a bold geometric sculpture that contrasts sharply with the surrounding architecture. The 28-foot-tall steel cube, painted in vibrant red and tilted on one corner, creates a sense of balance and tension, drawing viewers into its playful yet minimalist form. A circular hole in the center adds visual depth, offering a unique perspective of the buildings behind it.
